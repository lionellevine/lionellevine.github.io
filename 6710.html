    • Topics

  • This is the first semester of a year-long introduction to probability at the graduate level. The course assumes a knowledge of measure theory but some of the basic facts will be reviewed at the begining. We will follow Durrett's Probability: Theory and Examples (4th edition), covering these chapters:
    • Chapter 2. Laws of Large Numbers
    • Chapter 3. Central Limit Theorems
    • Chapter 4. Random Walks
    • Chapter 5. Martingales
